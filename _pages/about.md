I design machine learning and computer vision systems that hold up in real-world settings across both 2D images and 3D data.

During my PhD, I have worked primarily on medical ultrasound, with methods that transfer cleanly to other domains.
I start with users to align on problem definition, constraints, and success metrics, then drive delivery from raw data and labeling through training, validation, and maintainable deployment.

My background includes strong foundations in machine learning, computer vision, image analysis, and SLAM/SfM, and I am comfortable combining classical geometry with modern deep learning.
I am known for clear thinking, consistent follow-through, and straightforward communication.
